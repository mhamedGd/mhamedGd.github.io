Arcade Road Fighter

Arcade Road Fighter is a pixel style arcade game about fighting waves of enemy vehicles using a variety of vehicles and gadgets set in a desert environment, You can interchange between the cars and the different gadgets, I won't say the idea of the game is unique or different, but I will say that playing the game is fun and the concept is solid and easily scalable, It is easy to get into and not hard to be good at, The game was made by me and all the visual assets and programming was by me except for a single sprite by Kennly.nl, The sound effects were acquired legally and are credited inside the game, And all the music is royalty free, The Gameplay Video was tested on (Infinix Hot10i) a relatively low-mid phone, That's why there were some frame drops at the beginning of the video.

It is an android game and it does have a downloadable version!
